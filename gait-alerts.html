How gait detection works
Accelerometer + Gyroscope data from Apple HealthKit / Google Fit measures step timing, stride variance, and rotational sway.
Your personal baseline (set during calibration) is compared against live readings. Deviations are scored as a percentage.
When intoxicated, the brain loses fine motor control — causing wider stance, irregular step timing, and increased lateral sway.
